Turtle

General Information and Facts

     I'm going to talk about my favorite turtle species, Trachemys dorbigni, in Brazil it's called tiger-turtle, and in English it's known as D'Orbigny's slider. It can be found in Brazil, Uruguay and Argentina. It is omnivorous because it is an aquatic turtle, its diet consists mainly of vegetables, but they also need animal protein, so they hunt crabs, sponges and small fish and insects. It lives mainly in swamps, marshes, lakes, streams and rivers.

     Females lay, on average, more than 10 eggs per clutch. The eggs hatch between 2 to 4 months. A curious fact is that the sex of turtles is determined by the temperature of the nest during incubation: the lower the temperature, the greater the chance of being born male, and the higher the temperature, the greater the chance of being born a female. When they come out of their eggs, they measure an average of 3 cm, and reach a size of 25 to 30 cm in length in the adult stage, the females are generally larger than the males. Tiger tortoises live up to 30 years of age.
