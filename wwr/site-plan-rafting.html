Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share information and opinions for people who want to practice rowing. As a level of difficulty, places to practice, costs in general, accommodation, place to park, necessary equipment, teaching both for beginners, as for the more advanced and everything else we can provide. All for the greatest possible ease to avoid unnecessary stress and encourage the practice of this wonderful sport. We count on the help of the community to provide their sincere comments, photos of the place and even more information, thus keeping everything updated and complete with the evaluations of the users themselves.

Audience

The target audience of this site are people who want to start and learn rowing, also those who already practice this sport who would like new adventures, new challenges and to help the rowing community to grow. We hope that users are between 18 - 60. However, all help is welcome from those who have retired from the sport but have experience and information to contribute. Most of them will already live close to a rowing place or where they have the culture of pursuing this sport. The people in general who enter the site have a good financial condition, accessing the site both by laptop and mobile devices.
